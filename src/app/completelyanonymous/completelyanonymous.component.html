I can be completely anonymous by following privacy best practices
People holding this misconception put too much faith in the ability of privacy tools, and often do not understand their fundamental limitations. In reality, while these tools can increase your level of privacy, there is no way to be certain that you are anonymous on the internet.

Lab Instructions


The idea of this exercise is for you to determine the complete path of a message starting from the client (either A, B or C) and ending at a server (either X, Y or Z). In essence, what you are doing is figuring out TOR circuits and in turn, deanonymizing a clients identity.

Click on the Generate TOR Circuits button.

Choose the difficulty level you want; In Plaintext version, the messages will not be encrypted while in the Encrypted version, the messages wiil be encrypted and you need to determine the circuits on the basis of the time it takes for the messages to appear on the nodes.

Select the pair of nodes you wish to observe traffic on and click on the Start Simulation button. If you wish to end simulation, click on the Stop Simulation button.
